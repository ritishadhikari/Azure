Azure Machine Learning:
    - Platform for Training, Deploying and Managing Machine Learning Models

Cognitive Services:
    - Suite of services developers can build AI solutions

Azure Bot Service:
    - Cloud Bases Solutions for developing and managing Bots


Challenges and Risks with AI:   
    - Bias can affect Results: (Fairness)
    - Errors may cause harm (Reliability and Safety)
    - Data could be exposed (Privacy & Security)
    - Solutions may not work for everyone (Inclusiveness)
    - Users must trust a complex system (Transparency)
    - Who's liable for AI-driven decisions? (Accountability)
        - Solutions must meet ethical standards that are clearly defined
